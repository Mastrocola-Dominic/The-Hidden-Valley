The Hidden Valley

Trails

Easy

This trails is great simple and easy. It is great for beginners, casual hikers, and people of all ages. It spans ~2mi with a pavilion and restrooms at the end.

Medium

This trails is a little longer and more challenging than the easy trail. It is great for more serious hikers that want more of a challange. It spans ~5mi with 2mi of uphill. Children and Elderly should avoid this trail unless they are confident and experienced

Hard

This trail with challange even the most physically fit hikers. It spans 20mi with extremely steep hills, narrow paths, and unstable ledges. Please do not take this trail unless you have the proper equipment.
